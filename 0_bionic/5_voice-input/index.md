**Voice Input** transcribes speech locally and sends the transcript into Bionic so that you can speak your ideas to the agent. Currently, Bionic supports Voxtral by Mistral AI, which is a performant multilingual realtime transcription model.

## Voice settings

Your first transcription may wait a bit while the model loads. For voice input to start faster each time, turn on **Auto load voice model** to keep the transcription model loaded.

1. Open **Settings** → **Voice**.
2. Toggle on **Auto load voice model**
