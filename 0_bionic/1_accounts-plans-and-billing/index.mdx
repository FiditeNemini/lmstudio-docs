title: Set Up Billing and Add Credits
description: Sign in, manage a personal or team plan, and choose who pays for cloud model use.
index: 1
---

Cloud models require an LM Studio account with available credits. Local and LM Link models do not use Bionic cloud credits, and can be used without an LM Studio account.

## Sign in

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Under **General**, find **Account**.
3. Select **Sign in** and finish signing in through your browser.
4. Return to Bionic.

## Add credits for personal use

<img
  src="/assets/marketing/docs/bionic-general-manage-plan.png"
  alt="Bionic Billing and Usage settings with Manage Plan highlighted"
  style={{ width: "100%", marginTop: "16px", marginBottom: "24px" }}
/>

1. In **Settings** under **Account**, select **Manage plan**.
2. You'll be redirected to your account on the LM Studio Hub. Navigate to **Cloud Inference** under Features to add more credits to your account.
3. Complete checkout and ensure the new amount is reflected under **Available Balance**.
4. Return to Bionic and open **Billing and Usage**. Refresh to ensure the new balance is reflected.
